Early Blight

Alternaria solani

Severity:
Moderate

A common fungal disease causing dark brown spots with concentric rings on leaves, leading to yellowing and defoliation.

Immediate Actions
  • Remove affected leaves and dispose of them away from garden
  • Improve air circulation around plants
  • Avoid overhead watering
Treatment Options
  • Apply copper-based fungicide every 7-14 days
  • Use preventive fungicides like chlorothalonil
  • Consider organic neem oil treatments
Prevention for Future
Practice crop rotation
